# CHINOKABE — Logo Generation Prompts

Image-generation prompts for the game logo, derived from the game's identity:
rhythm + hole-in-the-wall + pose-silhouette + arcade/neon. Built for
[chinokabe.com](https://chinokabe.com).

Best results with **Ideogram** or **Flux** for legible wordmarks; Midjourney /
DALL·E for the symbol-only variants.

---

## Primary prompt (full logo + wordmark)

Logo for a rhythm arcade game called **"CHINOKABE"**. The core concept: a flat
wall with a glowing human pose-shaped hole punched through it — arms raised in a
dynamic dance/strike pose. Bold, geometric, neon-on-dark aesthetic inspired by
Japanese arcade and rhythm games (Beat Saber / Dance Dance Revolution energy).
The wordmark "CHINOKABE" in a chunky, slightly futuristic sans-serif, with the
subtitle "BEAT THE WALL" small beneath it. Electric magenta and cyan neon glow
against a deep near-black background, subtle beat/equalizer bars as an accent
motif. Clean, high-contrast, vector-style, centered composition, suitable as an
app icon and website header. Minimal, iconic, memorable.

---

## Square app-icon variant (favicon / app icon)

App icon, 1:1 square. A single bold glyph: a neon human silhouette mid-pose
bursting through a cracked wall, magenta-to-cyan gradient glow on deep charcoal
background. No text. Flat, geometric, high contrast, instantly readable at small
sizes. Rounded-square format.

---

## Tuning knobs

- **Vibe** — swap "neon/arcade" for "minimal flat, two-color, Swiss poster style"
  for something cleaner and more brandable (scales better to favicon/print).
- **Colors** — magenta + cyan reads "rhythm game". For a more Japanese-minimal
  feel, try "vermilion red and off-white, sumi-e ink brush wall."
- **The wordmark** — if the generator handles text poorly, generate the symbol
  alone and add the "CHINOKABE" wordmark yourself.

## Style variants to compare

**Retro 8-bit**

Pixel-art logo for "CHINOKABE", a rhythm arcade game. Chunky 8-bit pixel
lettering, a blocky pixelated wall with a pixel human silhouette punched through
it mid-pose. Limited retro palette (magenta, cyan, black), CRT-scanline vibe.

**Sleek minimal / brand mark**

Minimal flat logo for "CHINOKABE". Two colors only (vermilion + off-white). A
single clean geometric mark: the negative space of a posing human figure cut
from a solid rectangle (the wall). Modern sans-serif wordmark beneath. Swiss
design, lots of whitespace, scalable vector look.
